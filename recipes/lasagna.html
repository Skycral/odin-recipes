Classic Lasagna

Description

Classic lasagna that will satisfy your every need.

Every layer is done with perfection which will make the whole family appreciate the taste.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Onion
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Cheese, ricotta and parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the meat with onion until the meat begins to brown
  2. Lower heat and add marinara sauce
  3. Make one layer of noodles and spread the meat sauce
  4. On top of the sauce, put cheese on
  5. Repeat those two steps until all layers are made
  6. Bake in owen until it has a brownish top layer
