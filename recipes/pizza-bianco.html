Pizza Bianco

Description

A delicious pizza which can be made by everyone.

It has a white base with toppings of bleak roe, creme fraiche and onions.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Creme fraiche
  • Red onion
  • Bleak roe
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Turn the dough into a circular base
  2. Add a medium thick layer of creme fraiche
  3. Put the pizza in the oven and let it become crispy but not burned
  4. Add the other ingredients and eat before getting too cold
