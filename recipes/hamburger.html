Juicy Hamburger

Description

A juicy hamburger that will melt in your mouth. The secret behind the juicy meat is hidden in the Worcestershire sauce added.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Egg
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix the ingredients in a large bowl using your hands
  3. orm the mixture into hamburger patties
  4. Grill patties until browned and no longer pink, about 5 minutes per side
